Let's play Rock, Paper, Scissors!

Rules of the game:

  1. Player 1, type your choice into the browser. Pick either rock, paper, or scissors. For example if you want to play "rock" you should type /rock
  2. Player 2, follow the same steps as above but put your answer after player 1. For example, if player 1 picks "rock" and player 2 picks "scissors", you should type /rock/scissors
  3. Let the computer tell you who wins!
  4. Want to play again? Repeat the steps above.

Can't be bothered typing? Player 1 can choose your answer using the links below:

  • Rock
  • Paper
  • Scissors
